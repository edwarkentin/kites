Kites

There are many different kinds of kites - not just the single-string type that Charlie Brown flew (or tried to fly). Some, like Charlie Brown's single-string kite, are at the mercy of the wind. If there is wind blowing, it will stay up, but there's not much else you can do with them except enjoy how beautiful they are. "Sport kites" have 2 or 4 strings, which allow you to control where they go in the air.
